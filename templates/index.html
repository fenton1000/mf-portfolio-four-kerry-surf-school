Surfing Lessons

Group Lessons - €50 per student

Group lessons are our most popular option and are provided in Kids groups (6 - 11 yrs), Older Childern (12 - 16 yrs) and Adult groups at beginner and intermediate level. All necessary surfing equipment including wetsuits and boards are provided with these lessons.

The lesson lasts for 90 mins and the surfing equipment can be used for a further 90 mins after the lesson.

Please note that a group lesson means that you will be learning alongside other students in a larger group of up to 10 people.

Private Lessons - €150

Private Lessons can be booked with one of our instructors for an individual or a small group of up to 4 students. This lesson format is ideal for a beginner or intermediate that would like a one to one learning environment or small groups requiring a tailored experience.

Private Lessons are also the only format in which we provide tailored lessons to more advanced students.

Private lessons are 1 hour long and the price does not include equipment hire.

Advanced Lessons

We typically find that as a more advanced surfer you will typically only come looking for a lesson occasionally if there is a particular skill you want to work on. Therefore we currently provide such lessons in the Private Lesson format only, as this one to one or small group format best meets the needs of such students.

Lesson Times and Booking

Group Lessons can be booked using our online booking system or by emailing or calling us. Private Lessons must be booked over the phone.

Lesson Times will vary throughout the season. Group lesson times can be viewed through our booking system. Or please give us a call and we can let you know our availability for all lesson types on your preferred date.
